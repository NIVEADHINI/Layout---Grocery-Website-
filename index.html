Frequently Asked Questions

1.) Do You offer free delivery? ▼
Yes, we offer free standard delivery on all orders above Rs. 5,000. Express and scheduled delivery options are also available at checkout.
2.) How long will it take to receive my order? ▼
Delivery times vary based on your location and the product's availability. Most orders are delivered within 5–10 business days. Custom-made items may take longer.
3.) Can I return or exchange grocery after purchase? ▼
Yes, you can return eligible items within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original condition. Please check our return policy for details.
4.) How Can I contact customer support? ▼
You can reach us via live chat, email at support@funishop.com, or call our helpline at 1800-123-4567 (Mon–Sat, 10 AM – 6 PM).
